The Nasdaq and S&P 500 rallied to record high closes on Wednesday, fueled by gains in Nvidia and other Wall Street heavyweights ahead of inflation data and quarterly earnings reports due this week.It was the Nasdaq's seventh straight record-high close and the S&P 500's sixth straight.
