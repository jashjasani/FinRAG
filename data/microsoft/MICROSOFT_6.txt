June 2024’s second wave of games has arrived for the Xbox Game Pass. Microsoft is adding 7 new games as part of the second batch. They are EA Sports FC 24, Still Wakes the Deep, My Time at Sandrock, Keplerth, SteamWorld Dig, SteamWorld Dig 2 and Robin Hood- Sherwood Builders.Here is a brief look at the new games that have come to Xbox Game Pass.Still Wakes the Deep (Available on Cloud, PC, and Xbox Series X|S)Story continues below AdvertisementRemove AdStill Wakes the Deep has been available on Xbox Game Pass from day one. In the survival horror game, an “unknowable horror” hunts you on an oil rig in the North Sea.My Time at Sandrock (Available on Cloud, Console, and PC): Available from June 19My Time at Sandrock is set in a post-apocalyptic world 300 years after the Day of Calamity. In this farm life simulator, you’ll have to gather resources to build machines, since all technologies were destroyed on the Day of Calamity. Forge friendships in Sandrock and defend the desert community from monsters.
